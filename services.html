The NEZ Auto Mechanic Process

How we ensure 100% satisfaction and zero surprises.

01

Digital Diagnostic & Check-In

Once you drop your vehicle off, our technicians immediately perform a comprehensive digital multi-point inspection. We visually examine all major components, run advanced computer diagnostics, and pinpoint the exact root of your problem.

02

Transparent Estimate Review

Before any work is performed, you receive a detailed, itemized estimate straight to your phone or email. We take the time to explain exactly what is urgent, what can wait, and what the best approach is for your budget.

03

Expert Repair & QC Testing

Our ASE-certified mechanics go to work using only OEM or premium aftermarket parts. Once repaired, your vehicle goes through rigorous Quality Control testing, including test drives, to confirm the issue is completely resolved.
