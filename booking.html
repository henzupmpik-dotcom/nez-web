Appointment FAQs

What if I don't know exactly what is wrong with my car?

That is completely fine! Simply select "Engine Diagnostics" or "General Inspection" when you book your slot. Write down any symptoms (e.g., "knocking noise when I brake", "car pulls to the left") in the appointment notes, and our experts will diagnose it when you arrive.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Absolutely. When you book, you will receive an automatic confirmation email from our system containing a link to modify or cancel your appointment. We kindly request that you provide at least 24 hours notice to allow other customers to use that time slot.

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This allows us to complete your vehicle check-in and get the keys handed over so the mechanic can start working right on schedule.

Do you offer loaner vehicles or a shuttle service?

For repairs estimated to take more than 4 hours, we offer a complimentary shuttle service within a 10-mile radius of our shop. Rental cars can also be arranged at a heavily discounted rate if your car needs to stay overnight. Please notify us of this requirement in the booking notes if possible.
